🌾 Major Crop Diseases

🍅 Tomato Diseases

1. Leaf Curl Virus
Cause: Virus spread by whiteflies
Symptoms: Upward curling leaves, stunted growth
Treatment: Use insecticides, resistant varieties

2. Early Blight
Cause: Fungus
Symptoms: Dark spots with rings
Treatment: Fungicide spray, crop rotation

🥔 Potato Diseases

1. Late Blight
Cause: Fungus (Phytophthora infestans)
Symptoms: Rapid leaf decay, black lesions
Treatment: Copper fungicide, remove infected plants

2. Black Scab
Cause: Soil fungus
Symptoms: Black patches on tubers
Treatment: Use certified seeds

🌽 Corn Diseases

1. Rust
Cause: Fungus
Symptoms: Reddish pustules on leaves
Treatment: Fungicides, resistant hybrids

2. Leaf Blight
Cause: Fungus
Symptoms: Yellowing and drying leaves
Treatment: Proper irrigation, fungicides

🍎 Apple Diseases

1. Apple Scab
Cause: Fungus
Symptoms: Dark lesions on fruit
Treatment: Fungicides, sanitation

2. Powdery Mildew
Cause: Fungus
Symptoms: White powdery coating
Treatment: Sulfur sprays

🌾 Wheat Diseases

1. Rust
Cause: Fungus
Symptoms: Orange spores
Treatment: Resistant varieties

2. Smut
Cause: Fungus
Symptoms: Black powdery grains
Treatment: Seed treatment

🌱 General Prevention Tips

  • Use certified seeds
  • Maintain proper irrigation
  • Apply fertilizers correctly
  • Monitor crops regularly
  • Use pesticides responsibly
